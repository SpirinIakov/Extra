Why BJJ?

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) is a predominantly ground-based martial art, that uses the principals of leverage, angles, pressure and timing, as well as knowledge of the human anatomy, in order to achieve a non-violent submission of one’s opponent.
BJJ can be done by anyone, it was designed and developed so that a smaller, weaker person can defend his or herself against, or even defeat, a larger and stronger opponent, regardless of age, size, gender or physical limitation.
In addition, it's a great full body workout.

Services

Kids Classes

We are very serious about children’s safety and make sure that kids are treating each other with respect.
Our classes will teach them valuable skills that will stick with them for life. Skills such as self discipline, confidence, self defense, body awareness, and to be great movers.

Adult Classes

All our classes start with a vigorous conditioning warm up and then instructors move on to demonstrate techniques, set ups and positions.
Students then drill these and use them in the sparring that follows.
Sparring is not included in our beginner classes but is usually included in all others.

Equipment

Our trainings are with gi but, if you’re new to BJJ, you can rent or purchase it from our store.
We have a huge variety of custom designed Brazilian Jiu Jitsu gis, all of our BJJ gis have been hand selected from the best gi brands with performance, comfort, design, and durability in mind.
